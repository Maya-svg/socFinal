My coat

It rips, bleeds, tears and repairs but with a cost
It leaves a scar, making
a permanent reminder of the pain I suffered

Its personality is weird
its as fragile as a flower
but tough like nails
keeping the ugly within
I get judged based on my coat
harassed, ridiculed
all because of my coat

It glistens when wet
and tingles when I’m cold
it’s part of me
reacting to emotions and the world around me
the skin I wear represent the struggles black people have gone through
I am a reminder of all the bad that happened in the world
I am a symbol that stands for a possible difference
my skin is not a color but a story
a story of struggle, a story of hardship, pain & suffering
I’m proud of my coat, the coat I wear I inherited
Its history is unforgettable and undeniable
When I wear this coat, I wear it proudly

I wear the struggles of my ancestors
I wear their courage
I wear their fight for a better life
